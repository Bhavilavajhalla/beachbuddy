Beaches of Goa

Baga Beach

Recreational Activities: Nightlife, water sports, shacks

Water Sports: Jet skiing, parasailing

Highlight: Famous for vibrant nightlife

Interesting Fact: Baga Beach hosts some of Goa's biggest parties!

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Calangute Beach

Recreational Activities: Family-friendly, shopping, relaxation

Water Sports: Boating, jet skiing

Highlight: Known as the "Queen of Beaches"

Interesting Fact: Calangute is the largest beach in North Goa.

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Anjuna Beach

Recreational Activities: Flea markets, nightlife

Water Sports: Banana boat rides, windsurfing

Highlight: Known for its vibrant flea markets

Interesting Fact: Anjuna Beach was a hotspot for hippie culture in the 1960s.

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Palolem Beach

Recreational Activities: Kayaking, relaxation, beach walks

Water Sports: Kayaking, boat rides

Highlight: Serene crescent-shaped beach

Interesting Fact: Known for its calm and peaceful environment

Best Time to Visit: November to March
